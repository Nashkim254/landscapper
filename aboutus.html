About Us

Who we are

We are a leading company in delivering the best home and institutions’ interior designs, office and events decor and exceptional landscaping designs. Our markets traverses both locally and internationally.

Queen Evolve Interior Designs came into being in 2013 and got registered in 2020 in Kenya. The company is currently based in Kenya and offer its exceptional services both locally and internationally.

We provide pocket friendly customized interior designs, an ultimate solution to your floors, walls, lighting, and ceiling. We do this through a friendly consultation service and leave your home or institution totally transformed. You can also trust us for quality modernized curtains, seats, coaches, indoor swing chairs, and overall any office and home furniture of your dreams.

Vision Statement
