About Us

We are a leading organization in delivering the best interior home decor and designs and exceptional landscaping designs. Our markets traverses locally and internationally. Queen Evolve was born in Seychelles and is a Kenyan product.

During a visit to Seychelles, the founder and director of Queen Evolve discovered her inner gift in designs and landscaping. The unique tropical designs motivated her to transform most of the facilities she has lived in. She has offered the best consultancy services to thriving institutions and homes across the globe.

Being a Health Specialist, the founder and director of Queen Evolve Quality Solutions is additionally motivated to make waste gold. By use of Blue and Green Technologies, she would transform plastic and rubber waste into shoes. Her scientific intentions revolve around transforming the blue economy by collecting all plastic waste in oceans, rivers, dams, and lakes and transforming it into shoes. The goal of green technology is to reduce, recycle, renew and exercise environmental responsibility. It also aims at preserving the aquatic life and providing ambience for actualizing the sustainable development.
