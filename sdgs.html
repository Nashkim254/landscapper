Sustainable Development Goals {SDGs} that Queen Evolve Helps to Achieve

The sustainable development goals were adopted by the UN in 2015 and carries a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030, all people enjoy peace and prosperity. This organization will help Kenya support the achievement of the Big 4 Agenda, which revolve to achieve the following; food security, affordable housing, universal health care, manufacturing and Job creation.

Goal 2: Zero Hunger

Queen Evolve intends to end hunger by empowering thousands of women and girls in sustainable income generation by engaging them in employment and adaptation of green and blue technologies so they can feed their families and communities.

Goal 3: Good Health and Wellbeing

Queen Evolve intends to achieve to have invested in primary healthcare for all ages by 2030. It will also participate in programs to promote the universal healthcare (UHC) in Kenya and across the globe.

Goal 9: industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

By 2030, Queen Evolve will have built resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrilization and foster innovation. This will be achieved through green and blue technologies initiatives under this company.

Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

By converting plastic waste and rubber waste into shoes, Queen Evolve will participate in creating safe, resilient and sustainable human settlements and cities.

Goal 13: Climate Action

Queen Evolve will combat greenhouse gases emissions into environment by collecting all plastic wastes as plastics when exposed to heat and sunlight will release greenhouse gases.
