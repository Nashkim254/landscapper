Ms. Rhoda Jepotip
Founding Director & Chief Executive Officer

Rhoda is a woman leader, a coach and a mentor. She is an interior, exterior design, landscaping consultant and the vision bearer of Queen Evolve Quality Solutions. She brings positive transformation in every environment she sets her hands in. Rhoda has a great vision in helping achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and Big Four Agenda. Professionally, she is a Public Health Officer and Lawyer and is pursuing her Masters in Healthcare Management. Rhoda has worked both locally and internationally and have more than five (5) years work experiences both locally and internationally. She has lived with a mentally-ill brother and is passionate in building positive mental well being for parents, teachers, and workers. She is also motivated in building positive mental health among other coaching and training programs in schools, universities and colleges.

Her Belief; ‘It is Possible, it can be done, Just take the staircase towards it’.

Her Mantra; ‘Positive Attitude for Sustainable communities, cities and workspaces’.
